Portfolio Website for Artist — Case Study

A curated online gallery and booking system that led to a 200% increase in commission enquiries.

Overview

An independent visual artist required a refined online presence to showcase their portfolio, facilitate direct commissions, and enable gallery partnerships. We designed an elegant, image-first site with fast load times and an integrated enquiry workflow.

The Challenge

  • Existing site did not highlight artwork detail or scale images for high-resolution displays.
  • Weak contact flow resulted in lost inquiries and poor follow-up tracking.
  • Limited SEO presence reduced discoverability among galleries and buyers.

Our Approach

  1. Design-first layout with full-bleed imagery, responsive galleries, and lightbox viewing.
  2. Optimized image delivery using responsive srcsets and an image CDN for performance.
  3. Implemented a structured enquiry form and simple CRM integration for follow-ups.
  4. On-page SEO: descriptive metadata, structured data for artwork, and targeted content for galleries.
