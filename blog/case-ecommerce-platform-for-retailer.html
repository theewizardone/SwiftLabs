E‑commerce Platform for Retailer — Case Study

Scalable platform design and conversion-driven UX that delivered a 150% increase in online sales in month one.

Overview

A regional retail client engaged Swift Creations to replace a legacy store with a modern, secure, and high-performance e‑commerce platform capable of handling seasonal traffic spikes and improving conversion rates.

The Challenge

  • Slow page loads and a fragmented checkout experience were causing high abandonment.
  • Inventory management was siloed and manual, causing stockouts and oversells.
  • Marketing lacked reliable analytics and personalization capabilities.

Our Approach

We delivered a phased, measurable program focused on stability, speed, and conversion optimization:

  1. Discovery & analytics audit to identify friction points across the funnel.
  2. Re-architecture to a headless commerce model with CDN-backed static pages for product listings.
  3. Unified inventory and order management with automated sync to the backend ERP.
  4. Streamlined, one‑page checkout and optimized product pages with A/B tested CTAs.
  5. Integrated tracking and customer segmentation for targeted campaigns.
